Mechanics

Wardens

Behavior

When a Warden reaches 20% health (100HP), it gains a protective barrier which neutralizes arrows flying within five blocks of its head.

Additionally, it gains an invisible armor piece enchanted with Projectile Protection CCLV. Any attacks that bypass this barrier (Wind Charges, laggy Bow shots) will deal negligible damage.

Drops

The Warden loot table is as follows:

  • 100% One of the following:
    • 50% Netherite Ingot
    • 30% Reinforced Deepslate
    • 20% Silence Armor Trim
  • 100% Shard of Knowledge with one of the following enchantments:
    • 33% Efficiency VI
    • 33% Sharpness VI
    • 11% Protection V
    • 11% Looting IV
    • 11% Fortune IV
  • 100% Sculk Catalyst

Using a Shard of Knowledge

The Shard of Knowledge dropped from a Warden is used to upgrade a maximum-level enchantment, making it one level higher than its maximum. All possible encahntments are shown in the Warden loot table.

To apply a Shard, right-click while holding your equipment in your off-hand and the Shard in your main hand. The Shard of Knowledge will transfer its enchantment to the equipment and turn into a normal Echo Shard.

The equipment must already have the maximum-level enchantment for the Shard to apply. Shards of Knowledge are upgrades, not enchanted books. Additionally, due to technical limitations, you cannot hold a stack of multiple Shards for application. The application will simply fail.
